DISCRIPTION :
Battlegrounds Mobile India (BGMI, previously known as PUBG Mobile India) is a version of PUBG Mobile, exclusively available in India. It is an online multiplayer battle royale game by Krafton. The game was initially released on 2 July 2021 for Android devices, and on 18 August 2021 for iOS devices.
